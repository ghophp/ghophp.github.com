Description

This app was done, and still maintained for a small startup hosted in Joinville. Really cool customer to have, trying to succeed in the voracious world of startup. But this is not frightening for them, they are looking for big investors and this should be promising. We did a lot of tricks with Android to achieve this, such as listen for calls, SMS's and web data, synchronize all that into our server, and then, provide rich information for the user.
