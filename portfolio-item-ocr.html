Description

I am the team leader of the in-development software to Itajai's port responsible for track the entrance of the courtyard where the containers are stored temporarily before go to the ship or hit the road to all over the country.

Built in Qt with a local storage using SQLite, that is periodically synchronized with a cloud environment, the solution has multiple layers. On the basic set, the solution uses two cameras to take pictures from plate and container, those pictures are sent to the software that extract the characters. This is just a simple resume from a very complex process.

It is really awesome integrate hardware and software and see the results that can be achieved, seems like magic see the trucks crossing the gate and the plate and containers popping up at the screen. It is for sure one of the projects that I am most proud to be part of.
